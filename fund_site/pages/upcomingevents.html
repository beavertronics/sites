Events

Date Event Location Description
August 12th-16th Summer Camp Beaverton High School, Room 407, West Annex Each summer Beavertronics hosts a STEM based summer camp for middle schoolers. It is a half day camp from 9 to 12. Participants can learn Computer Aided Design drafting, engineering principles, lego robotics, and more.
Fall Fundraising Dinner + Auction Beaverton High School Cafeteria Occaisonally Beavertronics hosts a catered dinner to raise funds for the team. After dinner we showcase past robots and works in progress. Kids are welcome to come and may drive the robots.
January 2020 season kickoff! University of Portland, Beauchamp Gym, 7 AM The official FRC robotics season begins at the University of Portland where the challenge for the season is revealed. Promptly after kickoff, Beavertronics holds a strategy session at the shop in Room 407.
